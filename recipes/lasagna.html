Lasagna Recipe

Description: Lasagna is a layered Italian pasta dish made with sheets of pasta, a rich meat (or vegetable) sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta mixture, and plenty of melted cheese. It’s baked until golden and bubbling, creating a hearty, comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  • Make the meat sauce.
  • Cook the noodles.
  • Make the ricotta mixture.
  • Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  • Cover with foil and bake.
  • Let the lasagna rest before serving.
